What's New

Updates to the Katana Order Generator — an interim replacement for Katana AI while native functionality is rebuilt.

v1.2.1 7 March 2026 Latest

Bug Fixes

  • PDF prices and quantities are now read correctly. In some PDFs, products were being assigned the wrong unit price — prices appeared to shift down by one row in the order. Similarly, pack-size numbers in product descriptions (e.g. "box of 100") were sometimes being picked up as the order quantity. Both issues are fixed.
v1.2.0 6 March 2026

Improvements

  • More reliable PDF reading. The way the extension reads PDF purchase orders has been rebuilt. It now understands table structure properly, so each product line is read as a unit — the right quantity and price stay attached to the right product, regardless of how the PDF was created.
  • Fewer incorrect product matches. A matching shortcut that was occasionally linking products to the wrong lines in a PDF has been removed. Matches are now held to a higher standard.
v1.1.0 6 March 2026

Improvements

  • Better customer matching. The extension now searches company names and contact names in Katana — not just the display name — so customers are found more reliably when the PO uses a slightly different form of their name.
  • Fewer false matches. Product matching is now more strict, reducing cases where a product name or SKU accidentally matched unrelated text in an email or PO document.
  • More PO reference number formats recognised. Common variations in how customers write their reference numbers are now picked up automatically.
v1.0.0 5 March 2026 First Release
