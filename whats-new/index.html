What's New

Updates to the Katana Order Generator — an interim replacement for Katana AI while native functionality is rebuilt.

v1.2.4 9 Mar 2026 Latest
Bug fix — size and colour variants (SM, MD, LG, etc.) now correctly matched when the customer writes a product name with a size in parentheses, regardless of how the SKU is formatted.
v1.2.3 9 Mar 2026
  • Improved size variant disambiguation using SIZE_NORM lookup table
  • New tip shown when items are matched by name rather than SKU, prompting users to include SKUs for more reliable results
v1.2.2 9 Mar 2026
Bug fixes — improved PO number detection, delivery date detection, and matching of products with multiple size or colour variants.
v1.2.1 7 Mar 2026
Bug fixes — improved accuracy when reading prices and quantities from PDF purchase orders.
v1.2.0 6 Mar 2026
Bug fixes — further improvements to PDF parsing reliability.
v1.1.0 6 Mar 2026
Improvements to customer matching, product matching accuracy, and PO reference number detection.
v1.0.0 5 Mar 2026 First Release
  • Paste a customer email or upload a PDF to create a sales order draft
  • Automatic matching of products, customer, PO reference, and delivery date
  • Review and edit everything before sending to Katana
  • Manually search and add any products the automatic matching misses
